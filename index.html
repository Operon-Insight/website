About Us

We Are Professionals

OPERON is an advisory firm licensed and registered with the Board of Valuers, Appraisers and Estate Agents; and the Ministry of Finance bearing registration no. (VE(1)0304)

OPERON consists of professional and qualified property consultants with accumulated hands-on experience of more than 50 years in real estate and financial applications

OPERON provides innovative and client-oriented solutions, insights, advisory and consultancy services related to real estate industry

OPERON offers assistance to Malaysian-based Government-Linked Companies and private companies seeking to explore and engage in real estate business activities locally and globally
